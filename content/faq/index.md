### What?

'Leave X - Protect Democracy' (https://leavex.eu/) is a citizen-led initiative created to raise awareness of the dangers of disinformation campaigns and their impact on democratic societies. We have statistics of politicians usages of the social media X and an [open letter](/letter) for European politicians to Leave X, wich had a [support](https://openpetition.eu/leavex) of **3,230 signatures** across Europe.
