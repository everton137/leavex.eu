What? #

‘Leave X - Protect Democracy’ (https://leavex.eu/) is a citizen-led initiative created to raise awareness of the dangers of disinformation campaigns and their impact on democratic societies. We have statistics of politicians usages of the social media X and an open letter for European politicians to Leave X, wich had a support of 3,230 signatures across Europe.
